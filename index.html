About Year Up United
Closing the Opportunity Divide — one young adult at a time.
Year Up United is a workforce development organization that connects motivated young adults to the skills, experiences, and professional networks they need to launch high-quality careers. Through an intensive, employer-aligned training model, participants complete technical and professional skills development before moving into paid corporate internships — supported throughout by Year Up United coaches and staff. The result: work-ready, skills-first talent that shows up prepared, motivated, and ready to contribute from day one.
About the Event
A morning built for the future of work.
Corporate Partner Day is Year Up United's signature employer engagement event — bringing together corporate leaders, Skills-First talent, and workforce advocates for a morning of connection, insight, and shared purpose.

Whether you're a current partner deepening your commitment or exploring what a skills-first hiring strategy can unlock, this event is designed to make the value concrete, personal, and actionable.
